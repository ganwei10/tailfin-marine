Executive Summary

Global recreational fishing is undergoing structural growth: participation is hitting record highs, and spending is shifting from "entry-level gear" to "experience upgrades." Yet the long-overlooked "Clean & Cook" stage is emerging as the next frontier of category innovation. Today's market is dominated by passive folding fillet tables (an HDPE surface + faucet + knife slots), forcing users to carry and manually operate separate scalers, knives and vacuum sealers — fragmented, bulky, and impossible to run off-grid.

TailFin Marine has created an entirely new category: the Powered, Modular Catch & Cook System. Built on a unified base platform with a central-bay dual-slot stack, the scaler, electric fillet knife, vacuum sealer, water recirculation and off-grid battery are all plug-and-play. Customers buy the base unit once, then stack modular upgrades as their fishing style and budget evolve — creating a sustainable cycle of repeat purchases and value growth.

Core promise · Catch. Clean. Cook. Anywhere. — one system that turns "bringing the fish home" into "processing and preserving it right where you caught it."

About TailFin Marine

TailFin Marine is an outdoor-gear brand focused on the "post-catch processing" category. Founded by a team of fishing-obsessed engineers and product designers, we saw that the fillet tables on the market are just "a table," while the real pain points — powered processing, freshness sealing, and off-grid power — remained fragmented. So we built a "central-bay modular" architecture that compresses the entire Catch & Cook workflow into one portable, packable system.

We operate on an integrated R&D–manufacturing–sales model with supply-chain reuse: core structural parts leverage the mature tackle / lithium-battery / vacuum supply chains of the Pearl River Delta, delivering professional-grade quality at highly competitive cost — enabling fast rollout and localization across global markets.

We are not selling "a better table" — we are defining a new category. That is precisely the opportunity for distributors to claim an empty shelf and build differentiation.

Architecture & Core Tech

  • Central-bay dual-slot stack (blind-mate alignment): Standard's base drawer bay stacks into Pro by adding 4 modules; power, signal and structure align in one motion — hot-swappable, no wiring.
  • 316 marine-grade stainless steel: scaling head and body pass a 72-hour salt-spray test — corrosion-resistant, clean cutting without damaging the flesh.
  • Wet/dry vacuum sealing ≥-75kPa: a hidden liquid-collection box protects the pump; paired with puncture-resistant bags to lock freshness and prevent cross-odor.
  • Off-grid power 24V 6S1P: BMS dual charging (110V AC + 12V vehicle), ≥2 hours per charge, hot-swappable.
  • Recirculating water + splash guard: PC splash shield + 5L/min recirculating supply — clean hands, no wasted water.
  • Unified data layer: pricing, promotions and availability managed from a single back-end source of truth, synced in real time between the storefront and e-commerce back office — easy for distributors to run localized operations.
